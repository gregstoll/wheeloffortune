About this project

This is a solver for cryptograms just based on the known letters. Simply enter the pattern of letters in the word with . or ? or * in spots where the letter isn't known. Spaces are ignored.

The word list is taken from Google Books Ngrams, specifically the 1-grams from the 20200217 release.

The source code is available on GitHub. See my writeup of this project.
